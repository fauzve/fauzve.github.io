" My lovely Friends "

Friends

In a world of endless journeys we roam,
Amidst the chaos, we find a place called home.
Where friendships blossom and forever last,
A tapestry of memories from the past..

Friends, like stars, shine through the darkest night,
Guiding our hearts with their gentle light.
They lend a hand when we stumble and fall,
And lift our spirits,mending every wall.

Through laughter and tears,
they stand by our side,
In every triumph and when we feel tied.
They listen to our dreams, hopes, and fears,Embracing us, wiping away our tears.

With their warmth, they color life's dull days,
Painting rainbows in the most vibrant ways.
A symphony of laughter fills the air,
In their company, worries become rare.

Friends celebrate the essence of who we are,Accepting us,flaws and all, from afar.
They see the beauty hidden within our soul,
And their love makes us feel truly whole.

Through shared adventures,
the bond grows deep,In their presence, our hearts joyfully leap.
With each passing day, our friendship will bloom,
A treasure cherished, dispelling all gloom.

So let us raise a toast to friends so dear
,The ones who bring us endless cheer.
For in this journey, we're never alone,
With friends, our hearts forever entwined, known.
